Welcome and greetings!

This is the site for the gaming society at RGU, where a community of enthusiastic and passionate gamers—cough nerds—come together to chat about and enjoy a wide variety of games. From classic card games to the latest video game releases, we celebrate all forms of gaming.

Our society offers an array of activities and events, including tournaments, gaming nights, and workshops, providing something for every type of gamer. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a casual player, you'll find a friendly and inclusive environment where you can connect with like-minded individuals.

Join us to experience the excitement of gaming and make new friends along the way. We value teamwork, sportsmanship, and respect, and we're always looking to welcome new members into our vibrant community. Let's play!

Please feel free to look around our site, which has information about game nights, special events, and places for retail therapy.
